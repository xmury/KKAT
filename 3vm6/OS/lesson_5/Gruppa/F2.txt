Computer and its History
Computers appeared in people's life not long ago. In the middle of the 20th century common people had no idea about them.
In 1951 the first commercially available computer was introduced. In 1975 personal computers appered.
The Role of Computers in Teens' Lives
Teenagers use computers for many purposes. Firstly, they play computer games, watch cartoons and film. 
Secondly, they do school tasks, read books and find various information on the Internet.
A compatively new trend if communicating online. Such applications as Skype give an opportunity to talk to people who may be very far away
in real time.
